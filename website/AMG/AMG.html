AMG Models

Mercedes-AMG 2012

Power from the 6.2-liter V-8 is unchanged. In standard spec, it puts out 451 hp and 443 lb-ft of torque; as before, the Development Package adds 30 hp (481 total) and raises the speed limiter from 155 to 174 mph.

Mercedes-AMG 2015

The C is a rear wheel drive 4 door with 5 seats, powered by a 4.0L TURBO V8 engine that has 375 kW of power (at 5500 rpm) and 700 Nm of torque (at 1750 rpm) via a Seven-speed Automatic.

Mercedes-AMG 2018

The 2018 Mercedes-AMG C63 S packs a 503-horsepower punch from its beefy V8 biturbo engine. The chassis remains composed even under pressure and boasts gobs of grip. In its Comfort mode, the C63 S is a well-appointed and understated daily driver.

Mercedes-AMG 2023

The 2023 Mercedes-AMG C63 S comes with a hand-built, twin-turbo 4.0-liter V-8 engine that makes 503 horsepower and 516 lb-ft of torque. A nine-speed automatic routes power to the rear wheels; all-wheel drive is not an option
